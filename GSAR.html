Overview

The Global Supplier Add Change Request (GSAR) is the formal process for adding a new supplier or updating an existing supplier within Cummins’ master supplier system Supplier 360 (S360). It ensures data accuracy and compliance by requiring appropriate verification and documentation directly from the supplier.

Getting Started

  1. The requester identifies a GSAR Super User for their location using the internal contact list.
  2. The requester contacts the Super User and specifies whether they need to add or update a supplier.
  3. The Super User validates the necessity of the request in S360 before proceeding.

Types of GSAR Requests

  • New Supplier GSAR – onboarding a brand‑new supplier.
  • Changes to Existing Supplier GSAR – modifying records of an already‑approved supplier (internal or external).

New Supplier GSAR

Before a Super User can open a new‑supplier request, the requester must supply:

  • Supplier’s country
  • Whether the supplier is Indirect (non‑production) or Direct (production)
  • Commodity / Category
  • Requested Payment Terms (with justification if different from Cummins standard N90)
Required Documents
  • Letterhead (mandatory) – see content requirements below.
  • Tax Document – required only for certain locations:
    • United States: Signed W‑9 (dated < 1 year)
    • India: PAN Card
    • Other regions: VAT / GSTIN / Tax ID as applicable
Letterhead Content Checklist

Indirect Suppliers

  • Legal supplier name
  • Taxpayer ID / VAT #/ PAN / GSTIN / CNPJ (as applicable)
  • Purchase‑order mailing address & PO contact
  • Payment mailing address & A/R contact
  • Currency & complete banking details
  • Remittance notification email

Direct Suppliers

  • All of the above, substituting Ship‑From Address for Purchase‑Order Address

Changes to Existing Supplier GSAR

A change request can be internal (no supplier documentation) or external (documentation required).

Internal Changes (No Docs)
  • Payment terms (include business justification)
  • Global Entity add (make a direct supplier available to all plants)
  • Site or Supplier inactivation
External Changes (Docs Required)
  • Name Change – old vs. new name on letterhead; must match tax docs
  • Address Change – specify old & new address, address purpose, any new contacts
  • Address Addition – new purchasing/ship‑from address + linked payment address
  • Contact Change – old vs. new PO or A/R contact data
  • Contact Addition – new contact + address association
  • Banking Change – full new banking details (see template)
  • Currency Change – old vs. new currency noted

Important Notes

  • All documents must be signed (physical or e‑signature), dated within 12 months, and include the name & title of the supplier representative.
  • Quotes, invoices, blank letterhead, or email screenshots are not acceptable substitutes.
  • A change to Tax ID requires creation of a New Supplier GSAR instead of an update.
